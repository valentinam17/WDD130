Overview

Purpose

My rafting company is growing, and I want to provide rafting services to those who enjoy the outdoors and adventures. We provide water rafting services for the people seeking recreational activities by providing high quality customer service. This website will be a guide for them to know what to choose for specific groups (i.e. couples, families, friends get together), prices, and more.

Audience

My main audience tends to be families and indiviuals that go on vacation and they are in search of a rafting company serving this local community. Our customers are adventure seekers who enjoy the outdoors and exploring new places. Some are new to this others are experienced therefore we want to keep this website simple and easy to navigate for everyone to use.
